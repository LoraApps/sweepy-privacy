# Privacy Policy for SweepIt

**Last Updated: December 7, 2025**

## Overview
SweepIt ("the App") is a photo gallery management application developed by LoraApps. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect
**None.** SweepIt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Permissions
The App requires the following permissions to function:

### READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO
- **Purpose**: To display your photos and videos within the app
- **Usage**: All media files remain on your device. We do not upload, copy, or transmit your media to any external servers
- **Storage**: Media is accessed directly from your device's local storage

### WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 and below)
- **Purpose**: To delete photos when you use the app's cleanup features
- **Usage**: Files are permanently deleted from your device when you confirm deletion. This action cannot be undone

### INTERNET
- **Purpose**: To display special event announcements and app notifications
- **Usage**: The app periodically checks for banner messages (e.g., holiday greetings, app updates). No personal data is transmitted
- **Data Sent**: None. Only a simple configuration file is downloaded
- **Frequency**: Checked once per hour, cached locally

## Data Storage & Processing
- **Local Only**: All operations occur entirely on your device
- **No Cloud Backup**: We do not back up your photos or app data to cloud services
- **No Analytics**: We do not use analytics or tracking services
- **No Third-Party Services**: The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data

## Data Deletion
When you delete photos using SweepIt:
- Files are permanently removed from your device's storage
- Deletion is immediate and irreversible
- No copies are retained by the App
- Files may be recoverable from Android's trash/recycle bin for 30 days (Android 11+)

When you uninstall the App:
- All app preferences and temporary data are automatically deleted
- Your photos (not deleted through the app) remain on your device

## Children's Privacy
SweepIt does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.
